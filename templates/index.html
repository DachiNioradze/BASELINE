ABOUT ME

The beginning

Ever since I was little, I loved watching basketball; I admired the superstars as they gracefully but yet effectively managed to surpass their opponents and score, not to mention that I was fascinated by their level of discipline and dedication to the sport. That’s what made me wish to be like them.

It eventually got to the point that my parents offered me to start playing at the age of 7, and well, that’s what I’ve been doing ever since. Unfortunately, along the way, due to the countless injuries, such as broken fingers, arm, etc, I started to get out of shape and was desperate for help. I needed someone to work out with me so I could be able to experience all of it once again.

Michael Jordan

Motive And Product

Sadly, physically nobody could consistently practice with me for a few months, so I had to do it all by myself. However, the majority of the content/advice that I found online were poor quality and didn’t work in reality. There wasn’t a place where all of the valuable information was put together. During the time when I wasn’t playing, due to the injuries mentioned above, I developed an interest in programming and learned some basics in python - coding became my new hobby.

However I haven’t created any major projects independently, which I always wanted to. Now, I’m able to combine both passions to build a website that helps basketball players find good drills, workouts, and tips all in one place, since I don't want others to experience the same..
